Who We Are

A true Italian dining experience in Slidell, Louisiana

Fratelli's Italian Grille, located in Slidell, Louisiana — on the North Shore of Lake Pontchartrain — is locally owned and operated. Immediately upon walking in, you are surrounded by the delicious aroma of Italian cooking. All dishes are prepared to order and are made with the freshest ingredients.

Fratelli's Italian Grille of Slidell was established in 2017 and has become the Italian restaurant of St. Tammany Parish and the surrounding areas. The owners work hard to give you the true Italian dining experience, and are at the forefront of this family-owned and operated Italian restaurant.

The next time you are looking for true Italian dining ambience, visit Fratelli's Italian Grille of Slidell, Louisiana!
