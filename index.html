Our Story

Built on Family & the Freshest Ingredients

Fratelli's Italian Grille was established in 2017 and has become the Italian restaurant of St. Tammany Parish and the North Shore. The owners work hard to give you the true Italian dining experience — from house-made pasta and hand-tossed pizza to slow-simmered ragù and fresh-grilled seafood.

Whether you stop in for lunch, a long wine-soaked dinner, or a family catering order for the big game, you'll be welcomed like family.
